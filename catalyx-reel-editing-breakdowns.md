## Assembly checklist (per reel, on your Mac)
1. Open the matching Canva cover → drop in your real bottle/plant photo → export the cover frame.
2. Drop your clips on the timeline at the times above; trim to the cut points.
3. Burn in the on-screen text per beat; add VO or trending audio.
4. Sync cuts to the beat; export 9:16 MP4.
Or let the skill do it: `run the reel factory on "<folder>" — <reel #>`.
