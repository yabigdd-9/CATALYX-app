This app now exposes Shopify-style storefront surfaces that can be mirrored into a Shopify theme, used as headless routes, or linked from QR codes and product packaging.

## Product Data

The storefront uses two existing data sources:

- `lib/catalyx.ts`: product education, stage logic, dose bands, mixing, storage, safety notes, and product roles.
- `lib/products.ts`: store-facing product IDs, prices, images, stock status, and cart data.
- `docs/shopify-products-import.csv`: draft Shopify product import file with handles, variants, SKUs, tags, and draft prices.
- `docs/shopify-product-metafields.csv`: Matrixify-style product metafields for QR, label, chart, and app-route metadata.
- `docs/shopify-product-asset-map.csv`: product-to-label and QR asset map for attaching images/files in Shopify.
- `docs/shopify-import-guide.md`: import notes and publish blockers.
- `docs/shopify-theme/`: Shopify Liquid sections for product label/QR actions and collection navigation.

Keep product IDs stable because cart, product pages, QR routes, and aliases rely on the same IDs:

- `ax-pro`
- `bx-pro`
- `root-x`
- `vital-x`
- `pk-x`

- `flush-x`

## Checkout Path

Customer flow:

1. Product card or product page calls `AddToCartButton`.
2. Cart state is stored through `lib/store.ts`.
3. `/cart` posts cart items to `/api/stripe/product-checkout`.
4. `lib/stripe.ts` creates a Stripe Checkout Session from product price data.

Current fallback behavior:

- If Stripe is not configured outside production, checkout redirects to `/cart?mockCheckout=1`.
- If production Stripe keys are missing, the API returns a configuration error.

## Preorder Capture

`components/PreorderSignup.tsx` currently stores leads in browser local storage under:

```text
catalyx-preorder-leads
```

Captured fields:

- email
- bundle
- customer type
- source
- captured timestamp

Recommended production wiring:

- Shopify customer tag: `preorder`
- Shopify customer metafields: `bundle_interest`, `customer_type`, `source`
- Email platform segment: `Catalyx preorder`
- Optional Supabase table: `preorder_leads`

## QR / Packaging Notes

Use `/feed-chart` as the default public QR destination. Product-specific QR codes can append query parameters:

```text
/feed-chart?product=ax-pro
/feed-chart?product=pk-x&source=bottle
```

Supported product IDs match the product list above. When a product is present, the page renders a product-specific QR header, bottle/product visual, supported stages, quick dose references by medium, and highlighted rows in the full chart. The optional `source` parameter is displayed for tracking-ready QR variants such as `bottle`, `box`, `insert`, or `retailer`.

## Labelled Product Photos

Product photos in the storefront are generated by `components/ProductVisual.tsx` from the locked label assets:

- Collection cards show the front locked label applied to a bottle mockup.
- Product detail pages show a front and rear labelled bottle pair.
- The product detail label section links front-label PDFs, rear-label PDFs, v17 feed charts, colour references, and the product-specific QR chart.

The source label paths come from `lib/catalyx-assets.ts`. Keep those paths aligned with the locked public assets under `public/locked-assets/catalyx`.
